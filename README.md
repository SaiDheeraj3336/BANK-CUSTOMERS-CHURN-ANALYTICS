# BANK-CUSTOMERS-CHURN-ANALYTICS
Customer churn is one of the biggest challenges in banking industry. An end-to-end Data Analytics project analyzes customer behavior using Python, MySQL, and PowerBI to identify key factors influencing churn. It features data cleaning, EDA, interactive dashboards, DAX measures and actionable business insights to support data-driven decision-making.

## 📄 Dashboard 3 — Business Insights & Recommendations

### Key Business Insights

- Germany shows the highest customer churn.
- Customers aged **35–50** are more likely to leave.
- Inactive customers have significantly higher churn.
- Customers with fewer banking products churn more frequently.
- Credit score and account balance influence retention.


### Business Recommendations

- Improve customer loyalty programs.
- Target inactive customers with personalized campaigns.
- Increase cross-selling opportunities.
- Enhance customer engagement strategies.
- Offer customized financial products.
